## Initial Problem Area

Our team is exploring several technology-related problems affecting university students and campus communities. These include attendance management, academic collaboration, and campus communication. We aim to identify a problem that can be solved through a practical and innovative IT solution.

## Target Users

- University students
- Lecturers and instructors
- Academic administrators
- Student organizations and campus communities

## Initial IT Venture Direction

### Idea 1: Smart Classroom Attendance System
A QR-code-based attendance platform that allows students to check in digitally while providing lecturers with attendance reports and analytics.

### Idea 2: Student Study Group Finder
A platform that helps students discover and join study groups based on courses, interests, and schedules.

### Idea 3: Campus Event Notification System
A centralized platform that informs students about campus events, deadlines, workshops, and announcements through personalized notifications.

The final project idea will be selected after opportunity scanning and user validation activities in Lab 2.
