# Customer Discovery Summary

## Respondents
15 respondents

## Repeated Pain Points
1. Manual attendance consumes class time.
2. Attendance records are sometimes inaccurate.
3. Students forget to sign attendance sheets.
4. Attendance sheets can be lost or damaged.

## Current Alternatives
- Paper attendance sheets
- Verbal attendance
- Spreadsheet records

## Validation Result
The problem appears to be real and recurring for students and lecturers. Most respondents expressed interest in a faster and more accurate attendance process.

## Next Step
Design and validate a QR-code-based Smart Classroom Attendance System MVP.
