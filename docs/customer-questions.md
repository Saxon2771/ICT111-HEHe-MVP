# Customer Discovery Questions

## Project Title

Smart Classroom Attendance System

## Target Respondent

Students, Lecturers, Academic Staff

## Consent and Context

1. May we ask you a few questions for our ICT111 course project?
2. Your participation is voluntary. We will not collect unnecessary personal data. Is it okay if we take notes?

## Recent Experience

3. Tell us about the last time attendance was taken in your class.
4. What were you doing at that time?
5. What happened from start to finish?

## Current Workaround

6. How is attendance currently recorded?
7. What tools or methods do you use?
8. What is difficult, slow, confusing, or frustrating about the process?

## Pain Severity and Consequence

9. How often do attendance-related problems happen?
10. How serious is this problem on a scale of 1-5? Why?
11. What happens when attendance records are incorrect?

## Closing

12. Is there anything else we should understand about attendance management?
13. May we use your anonymized response as part of our course project evidence?
