|----- | ------------ | ----------- | ------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Idea 1 | Manual attendance tracking is inefficient and prone to errors. | Students, lecturers, academic staff | Paper attendance sheets and verbal attendance checks | QR-code-based web attendance system |
| Idea 2 | Students struggle to find study partners and study groups. | University students | Messaging groups and social media posts | Student matching and study group platform |
